This is a book club for everyone who loves to get lost between the pages, discover new written adventures and share stories that moves and inspires us.

Each month, we choose a new book to read together. We share our thoughts and build our comunity through the stories we explore. It doesn't matter whether you're a fast or slow reader -this is a place for every kind of reader.

Book of the month:

Thy Soul Shall Bear Witness by Selma Lagerlöf

Selma Lagerlöf's 'Thy Soul Shall Bear Witness' is a captivating historical novel that transports readers to the Swedish countryside in the late 19th century. Through vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Lagerlöf explores themes of family, love, and faith against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society. Her lyrical prose and attention to detail create a rich tapestry of the characters' lives, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the setting and time period.
