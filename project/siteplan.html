Site Plan Project

Site Name

The Word Nook Book Club

This is an invented book club name that I created. I choose this name beacuse I wanted the club to be a familiar cozy place where we can share our opinions about our favorite books and share them with others.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about the book club: the books selected for each month with their reivews. It also provides the option of joining the book club and being a member.

Scenarios

What is the purpose of the book club? How can I join the book club? What books they are reading each month?

Color Schema

#5B1F0B: the color of the buttons and the headings.

#406441: the color of the background of the footer and the menu.

FFFFFF: the color of the whole page and the text where the background is #406441 or #5B1F0B

Typography

Playfair Display: headings and special text.

Roboto: for the paragraphs. Mainly in the body, where information needed to be provided.
